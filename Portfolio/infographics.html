Project Reflections

This projects align with LTD program goal #1 (objectives 1.1-1.3) because they demonstrates the ability to develop learning graphics that take complex ideas and visually represents them in a digestable format for learners. These projects were two of my earliest experiences creating digital images using Canva. I discovered that I love the ease of use that Canva allows users. It's easy to choose from a variety of templates and offers an interface that is easy to navigate and includes features that make graphic design easy for novice users like myself. I have continued to use Canva to create images and diagrams for a variety of purposes including game art, graphics for design documentation, and even the buttons for this website. In creating projects of this nature in the future, I would love to explore similar editors (such as Polotno Studio) and compare the features they offer users compared to Canva.
