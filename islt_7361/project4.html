Introduction and Learning Objective

The purpose of this lesson is to use Nearpod to introduce students to factors that enable geckos to adhere to surfaces. This is my final project for Introduction to Digital Media ISLT 7361.

Learning Objective

Students will be able to identify factors that enable geckos to adhere to surfaces when given several possible options.

Reflection

Incorporating this video in a Nearpod lesson helped me to learn more about Nearpod as a service to use in making interactive videos. I was able to find a new tool to help increase student engagement in video learning. This discovery has helped me to explore the potential for adding interactivity into lessons that I have already completed.
